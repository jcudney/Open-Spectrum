NOTICE

The content on TransCompass is intended for informational and educational purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and should not be used in place of speaking with a qualified mental health professional.

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, suicidal thoughts, or feel unsafe, please seek help immediately:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (U.S.): Call or text 988
  • The Trevor Project: Call 1-866-488-7386, text ‘START’ to 678-678, or visit https://www.thetrevorproject.org
  • Emergency Services: In an emergency, call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room

World Professional Association for Transgender Health

The World Professional Association for Transgender Health (WPATH) is an interdisciplinary professional and educational organization devoted to transgender health.

Website Email: wpath@wpath.org

Planned Parenthood

Planned Parenthood is a nonprofit healthcare provider offering inclusive services, including gender-affirming hormone therapy at many locations. It is committed to accessible care for all, regardless of identity or income.

Website Hotline: +1 (800)-230-7526

Plume

Plume offers gender-affirming hormone therapy via telehealth in most U.S. states. It is run by and for trans people and provides letters for surgery, name/gender changes, and more.

Website Hotline: +1 (720)-897-3749
