Trans Compass

This site was built from a simple belief: transgender people deserve better access to knowledge, support, and safety.

Whether you're just starting your journey, supporting someone you love, or educating yourself, you're in the right place. We’ve gathered the most reliable information we could find—and made it easier to explore, with care and clarity at every step.

At TransCompass, we believe that understanding is the first step toward acceptance. Our Trans Information section offers accessible and respectful guides to understanding transgender identities, experiences, and language—written to support both trans people and those who want to learn more.
