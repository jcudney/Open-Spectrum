About TransCompass

TransCompass is a digital platform created to help people navigate transgender topics with clarity, compassion, and confidence.

Whether you're trans, questioning, an ally, a parent, a healthcare provider, or simply curious—this site is for you.

Our Mission

To make trustworthy, accessible, and affirming information about transgender identities available to anyone who needs it—without judgment, barriers, or misinformation.

Millions of transgender people worldwide, and more than 1.6 million in the U.S. alone, lack access to clear, supportive, and accurate information about their identities, healthcare options, and legal rights. This is especially true for individuals in rural areas, young people, or those facing hostile or uninformed environments.

Too often, people seeking information about gender identity are met with confusion, outdated language, or harmful stereotypes. Finding accurate guidance—especially on topics like healthcare, legal rights, or coming out—can be overwhelming.

TransCompass is here to fix that
